Party in my Pocket

Come join us for a fun-filled booze-infused fiesta!

Overview

Got a cubbard of half-consumed Bacardi from that party where you woofed all over your christmas sweater? Maybe a chunking slurry of old Sweet Vermouth from your grandpa? Lack a sense of party creativity? You came to the right place! This one-of-kind application allows you to enter a list of alcoholic beverages in your possession with the ultimate goal of kick-starting a night of debauchery and merry-delight! If you are at all intrigued, please see the instructions below. Down the hatch!

Instructions

  • 1. Enter ingredients in search bar
  • 2. Click on ingredient from search menu
  • 3. Click submit button when ingredient list is complete
  • 4. Click button in ingredient list if you would like to remove a selection
    • 5. Browse and enjoy!
